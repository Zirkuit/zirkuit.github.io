Working at Zirkuit

Zirkuit is a group of open-minded and talented people that are working together on developing small and large projects using software and hardware combined to find solutions to modern-day problems.

As a member of our group, you will have the unique opportunity to shape our future, together with the brightest minds in their fields.

Core values
- Open to new ideas: We're an organization of people who are open to everyone's ideas. Everyone can express their ideas on our projects, with the same goal of developing the perfect product.
- Collaborative: We work and result in success together, and in collaboration.
- Growth-oriented: We focus on learning new things while working together, always growing and improving.

Who we're looking for
We compromise various nationalities, ages, and socioeconomic backgrounds. We're looking for people with diverse perspectives and backgrounds.

Zirkuit provides equal opportunity in all of our employment practices to all qualified employees and applicants without regard to race, color, religion, gender, national origin, age, disability, marital status, military status, genetic information or any other category protected by federal, state and local laws.
