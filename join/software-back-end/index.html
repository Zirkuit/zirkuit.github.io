Software, Back-End

Zirkuit is developing small and large projects using software and hardware combined to find solutions to modern-day problems. Do you love working on challenges that no one has solved yet? Do you like innovating?

As a member of our group, you will have the unique opportunity to shape our future with solutions to many common issues people are facing. At Zirkuit's Back-End Development team, you will have the chance to work with the brightest minds on mindblowing projects that aim to change the near future.

About the role
You will work on Zirkuit's Web Design team to help create promotional websites, as well as create web-based application Back-end's for Zirkuit projects. These contributions will lead to helping us improve human lives involving Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technologies. You will be responsible for the server-side web application logic as well as for the integration of the front-end part.

About you
- You find large challenges exciting and enjoy discovering and defining problems as much as solving them.
- You deliver. You may enjoy thoughtful conversations about problems and perfecting designs, but in the end, you know that what matters is delivering a manufacturable solution that works every time.
- You are a cross-disciplinary team member. You are excited to work with and learn from software, hardware, electrical and graphical designers. You are comfortable communicating across teams.

Duties and Responsibilities
- Be involved and participate in the overall application lifecycle.
- Main focus on coding and debugging.
- Collaborate with Front-end developers.
- Define and communicate technical and design requirements.
- Provide training, help and support to other team members.
- Build high-quality reusable code that can be used in thew future.
- Develop functional and sustainable web applications with clean codes.
- Troubleshoot and debug applications.
- Learn about new technologies.
- Stay up to date with current best practices.
- Conduct UI tests and optimize performance.
- Manage cutting-edge technologies to improve applications.
- Collaborate with multidisciplinary team of designers, developers and system administrators.
- Participate in conferences and educational programs.
- Follow new and emerging technologies.

Key & Preffered Qualifications
- In-depth understanding of web development.
- 1+ years of experience with programming languages like Java, Ruby, PHP and Python.
- 1+ years of experience with CMS framework.
- Familiarity with front-end languages such as HTML, JavaScript and CSS.
- Critical thinker and problem-solving skills.
- Good organizational and time-management skills.
- Good problem solving skills.
- Excellent communication skills, both written & verbal.
- Self driven and able to execute with limited supervision.

Education & Experience

What we offer

- Growth potential. We rapidly advance team members who have an outsized impact.
